About Us

The Stony Brook Game Developers Club is a club that demonstrates game making software such as Unity and Unreal Engine 4 and teaches about aspects of game design such as art design and narrative design. The club is a forum where aspiring game developers can meet and collaborate in developing games. The club regularly has guest speakers from the game industry speak about their game development process and about their current game projects. At the end of each spring semester, the club hosts the annual Stony Brook Game Developers Competition where teams of students showcase their games and a winner is selected among them. The club's meeting time is Tuesdays at 7 pm in New Computer Science 115.
